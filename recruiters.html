WHY LNMIIT?


The ​LNM Institute of Information Technology (LNMIIT), ​Jaipur is a joint venture of the Government of Rajasthan and the ​LUM (Lakshmi Niwas Mittal & Usha Mittal) Foundation headed by the world steel baron ​Mr. Lakshmi Niwas Mittal (Chairman Arcelor-Mittal). This institute has been serving the industry for over a decade.The institute is at par with all the NITs and IIITs when it comes to admissions. The students are admitted on the basis of JEE Mains scores and the cut offs are always higher than national qualifying score. Last year’s placement drive saw the arrival of some of the most sought-after companies like Microsoft, Amazon, Adobe, Sprinkler, Zomato, Samsung to name a few. Research internships were also offered to the students.

From 6 student selections in GSoC’16 to 15 in GSoC’17, the results are promising. Teams from the college are also participating in the ACM ICPC India regionals, Texas Instruments’ Innovation Challenge and IEEE technical projects every year including one selection in Outreachy by Wikipedia and Zulip Summer of Code.

The highest package of 27 LPA was offered by Amazon and a healthy average of 7.27 LPA was maintained with a placement percentage of 81.65%. Start-ups by our Students: Lucideus, HttpKart, Finoit, Neutrino IT, Zero Waste, Enuke, Playlyfe, Teramatrix etc.


LNMIIT has been accredited Grade ‘A’ by NAAC with a score of 3.15/4. According to Edu Rank, LNMIIT stands 3rd amongst the top private institutes of India and 85th amongst all the institutes by MHRD NIRF.

During the session 2017-2018, LNMIIT successfully organized TEDx LNMIIT. By bringing the versatility of thoughts and fusion of ideas in one room, TEDx LNMIIT experienced the journey of various speakers including Hussain Haidry, Ishi Khosla, Rohan Parikh, Sanjoy Roy, Santosh Das, Siddharth Agarwal and Sudhanshu Kaushik.


Recruitment Procedure

  • Company / Organization sends JAF (Job Announcement Form) containing details of the job offer (pay package) by post or email to Placement Cell / Office, LNMIIT (placement@lnmiit.ac.in).
  • If the company / organization wishes to conduct a Pre-Placement Talk (PPT) they can send a request along with the preferred dates.
  • The JAF is made available to the students, along with any other information furnished by company organization.
  • Interested students show their willingness to appear for the recruitment process of a company by signing its JAF.

Companies come to the campus on the dates they have filled in their JAFs and conduct tests and interviews according to their recruitment process.
