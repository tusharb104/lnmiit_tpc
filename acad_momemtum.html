Academic Momentum

At LNMIIT, students work hard to create a bright future for themselves and for the community. They study various subjects and sharpen their intellectual and work-related skills from basic English to managing complex computer networks to critical thinking of analytical problems. This rigorous process empowers them to pursue their interests in the academia or in industry. To this end, the institute and students have taken several initiatives, a few of which are listed below:

    Professional Societies


  • IEEE Student Chapter

    • IEEE has become a real communicator and facilitator amongst all the scientific and technical communities which are striving for the betterment of this world. As an information technology research institute, LNMIIT plays an important counterpart role in this process. LNMIIT has always been a keen activity organizer in areas related to technology and professional growth of an individual. The plethora of resources provided by IEEE help the students to keep pace with sharper edge towards excellence. Several workshops have been organized by the IEEE Student Chapter at LNMIIT.

  • Google Developer Group

    • Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are user groups for people who are interested in Google developement technology: everything from the Android and App Engine platforms, to product APIs like the YouTube API and the Google Calendar API, to initiatives like Open-social. GDG LNMIIT aims at bringing like-minded people together and giving them a platform to develop, brainstorm and interact among themselves. GDG LNMIIT invites everyone interested in Google’s gigantic technology, be it a student or a professional. In a world where we have our every question answered by Googling, everybody is invited to be a part, contribute, grow and learn.

  • The American Society of Mechanical Engineers

    • ASME is a not-for-profit membership organization that enables collaboration, knowledge sharing, career enrichment, and skills development across all engineering disciplines, toward a goal of helping the global engineering community develop solutions to benefit lives and livelihoods. ASME serves this wide-ranging technical community through quality programs in continuing education, training and professional development, codes and standards, research, conferences and publications, government relations and other forms of outreach.

  • Society of Automotive Engineers

    • SAEINDIA is India's leading resource for mobility technology. As an individual member driven society of mobility practitioners the ownership of SAEINDIA wrests with its members who are Individuals from the mobility community, which includes Engineers Executives from Industry, Government Officials, Academics and Students SAEINDIA is an affiliate society of SAE International registered in India as an Indian nonprofit engineering and scientific society dedicated to the advancement of mobility industry in India. Members can access vital high-tech mobility information through renewal registration fee for attending SAEINDIA National and International Congress.

  • Computer Society Of India

    • Computer Society of India (CSI) offers a range of technical and networking opportunities through workshops, seminars, courses and participation by major industries, sharing best practices and exchange of ideas and information. CSI holds annual technical fests with programming contests, quizzes, interaction with IT industry luminaries, career counseling and much more. Membership of CSI can be beneficial to students (especially those of Computer Science) in many ways and provides them exposure at National front.



    Summer Training Programs


  • Every year the institute offers an Industrial training to about 25 meritorious students from the 3rd year, at ArcelorMittal Steel Plant, Kazakhstan. The training is aimed at providing the students with an exposure to the Industrial environment at a global level.
  • Students go to various research labs and universities worldwide for summer projects. These include University of Manitoba, Canada; UAB, Spain; Western Michigan University, USA; INRIA, France; University of Cassino, Italy; ICCL Lab, CPU, Taiwan; Polytechnic University, New York, USA; University Of Warwick, UK; CBIA, Brno, Czech Republic; SUT, Thailand.
  • At LNMIIT, we facilitate student exchange programmes, under which students get an opportunity to spend a semester at elite institutions like Knoesis Centre, Ohio State University, USA; IIIT Hyderabad, IIT Gandhinagar and IIT Delhi.
  • Students have also done their summer training at renowned companies including TCS, CISCO, Microsoft, Ericsson etc.
  • Students get a chance to receive training at CERN, Switzerland, which is one of the world's largest laboratories founded by the European union. It is also the birthplace of World Wide Web (WWW).
  • Summer projects are also undertaken by the students at elite institutes including IITs, IISc Bangalore, IIIT Hyderabad, IIIT Delhi and BITS Pilani.
